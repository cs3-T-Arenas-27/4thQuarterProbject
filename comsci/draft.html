    • Dive into the mind's wonders. Discover cognitive neuroscience.

    Think of the brain as a central computer that controls all the body's functions.
    The rest of the nervous system is like a network that relays messages back and forth
    from the brain to different parts of the body. The functions of the brain is to regulate
    the majority of our body and mind purpose. This includes vital functions like breathing
    or heart rate, to basic functions like sleeping, eating, or sexual instinct, and even
    superior functions like thinking, remembering, reasoning, or talking. In order to carry-out
    any seemingly simple task, our brain has to perform thousands of processes to ensure that
    we properly complete the task.
